GULAB JAMUN

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Receipe

  1. Dough: Mix milk powder, flour, ghee, and baking soda. Add milk for a soft dough.
  2. Shape: Form dough into smooth balls without cracks.
  3. Fry: Heat oil, fry balls till golden brown
  4. Syrup: Boil sugar, water, cardamom. Soak fried balls in syrup.
  5. Serve: Let them soak for 1-2 hours. Garnish with nuts if desired. Enjoy!

KAJU KATLI

Ingredients

  • Cashew Nuts
  • Ghee
  • Sugar

Receipe

  1. Grind cashews into a fine powder.
  2. Make sugar syrup (one-string consistency) .
  3. Mix cashew powder into syrup until a dough forms.
  4. Add ghee and cardamom (optional).
  5. Shape, cool, and cut into pieces.
